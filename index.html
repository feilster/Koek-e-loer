About Us
(who are we and what do we offer)

We offer professionally customized novelty cakes for any theme or occasion. Whether it's tiered wedding cakes, themed birthday cakes or even just a batch of baby shower cupcakes, we've got the experience and expertise to create whatever you desire.

We've been in the novelty cake business for more than 15 years and during this time we have called some of Pretoria's most influential people our customers. We operate from our home in Centurion where we provide a personalized service.

Birthdays

Specializing in birthday cakes we cater for any theme and any age. Be it a spiderman cake for a 4-year-old or a Harley for a 40-year-old, we've got you covered.

Weddings

For that special day we construct any design, from the very simple and elegant to a multi-tiered masterpice.

Parties

To liven up any party we provide cakes and cupcakes to suit any theme.

Special events

From baby showers to anniversaries, kitchen teas to holy communion, and any celebration in between, our product will add the finishing touch.
