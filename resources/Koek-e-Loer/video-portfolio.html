Sight - Video Case





  • Sight

    The company approached us with the need for a new website that accentuated their extraordinary brand. Instead of repurposing content from their old site, we scrapped everything and worked with Optimo to produce content that represented the undeniable quality of their products.

    Our mission was to find the best way to showcase their current collection. We worked with product photographer Peter Parker to capture the superior quality of finishes, timeless shapes and gorgeous colors of each product. After countless hours of image production and development, the results appear on the website, weightlessly floating above a shadow that is dynamically drawn in Flash.

    Throughout the website you'll come across videos that convey the history, the quality of materials and what truly makes their brand unique.
